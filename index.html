Paper Presentation:

It’s that time of the year to witness the battle of brains. Novelty is a virtue. We, the Paper Presentation team of Paradigm 2k15 invite papers proposing novel and non-trivial ideas and research works in any CSE domain. It provides the authors an outstanding opportunity to unleash the genius in them and as well learn a lot by interacting with the like. Show the world your research! Let them know who’s the next Einstein!!


The rules are as follows:
  1. Only CS domains are encouraged.
  2. Maximum of 3 authors and only student authors allowed. It is not a necessity that all the authors must be from the same institute.
  3. We take plagiarism very seriously. Only original research works are accepted.
  4. The paper must not have been presented or published anywhere else.
  5. The paper must be in two column IEEE format. This format must be strictly adhered to. The paper length must be limited to 6 pages.
  6. The paper must be in .pdf or .doc or .docx formats.
  7. The paper must be sent through mail. The instructions are as follows:
    1. The subject must be the title of the paper.
    2. The body of the mail should include - Name and Institute of participant (starting from the first author and going till the third one), Department and Year, Contact numbers, Email addresses.
    3. Send the paper as an attachment with this mail.
    4. The email id is : paradigmpaper2k15@gmail.com

Last date to send in the paper: 27th August 2015

Acceptance notification: 30th August 2015

Triathlon

​ RULES
  • Each team MUST consist of THREE members.
  • Lone wolves and duos are not permitted. Sorry!

PS: If you don’t have a team, we can find you one ;)

ROUNDS

Prelims ​ :
  • A set of not-so-technical-not-so-non-technical MCQs to be solved in30minutes.
    Crack as many as you can to get through the prelims.
  • Top 12 teams qualify to the finals.
Finals ​ :

It consists of 3 legend-wait-for-it-dary rounds that test your mettle as a Programmer. As they say, you’ll never know how far you can go unless you ​ Tri ​ . BAZINGA!

First Round ​ - ​ One on One Connexions Face off .
  • Time limit -​ 30​ minutes.
  • 12 teams will be split to 6 groups each containing 2 teams.
  • It will be a ​buzzer round​ between two teams of a group.
  • The team that cracks the most number of correct answers among the group of two will qualify to the second phase of finals.

So, ​ 6​ teams proceed to the next level.

Second Round ​ - ​Code Obfuscation and Reverse Coding.
  • Obfuscated codes will be displayed on the monitor and the teams are required to figure out the code’s logic correctly.
  • Object file (.obj or .class) will be shown and teams need to predict the program’s logic.
  • Selection will be based on the correctness of answers. In case of a tie, speed of completion will be considered

Out of 6 teams,​ 4​ teams enter the final round.

Third Round ​ - ​Core Programming
  • Finalists will be given a set of problems for which they have to produce the code within 30 mins. Feel free to use a programming language of your choice.
  • The top 3 teams will walk away with the cash prizes. Do you dare to​ Tri ​ ? :P
